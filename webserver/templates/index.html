This applicaiton was inspired by 'recapbot' - created during the London TechCrunch Disrupt Hackathon 2016. The original bot took the conversation from a Spark chatroom and processed the messages as being 'on' or 'off' topic, returning a markdown where 'off' topic comments were suppressed, and 'on' topic comments were enlarged.

We now go a step further and summarise the contents of a chatroom into key topics in the conversation. Each topic is displayed as a wordcloud, leading you to all the messages related to that topic to give you a neat, visual overview of all the discussions in the chatrooms. Sign in to your Spark account and give it a try!
